Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is DinaKart (DAN)?

DinaKart (DAN) is a utility token designed for the nashop.store ecosystem, providing users with rewards and benefits within our platform.

When will the mainnet launch?

The mainnet is scheduled to launch in 2026. We are currently in the development phase and will provide regular updates through our official channels.

Which blockchain is DinaKart built on?

DinaKart (DAN) is built on the Polygon network, chosen for its scalability, low transaction costs, and robust security.

Token & Trading

How can I buy DAN tokens?

Once launched, DAN tokens will be available for purchase through our token page at token.nashop.store. We will announce all official purchase channels through our social media.

What are the benefits of holding DAN tokens?

DAN token holders will receive various benefits including:

  • Platform rewards
  • Exclusive discounts on purchases
  • Participation in platform governance
  • Access to special features and promotions

Security & Technical
